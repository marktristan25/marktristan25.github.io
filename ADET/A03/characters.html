Meet Some Of The Major Characters!
Finn the Human

The brave human boy who dreams of becoming a great hero across the Land of Ooo.

Jake the Dog

Finn's loyal, shape-shifting dog companion with a laid-back attitude and a big heart.

Princess Bubblegum

The smart and compassionate ruler of the Candy Kingdom, master of science and candy!

Marceline

The free-spirited vampire queen who rocks hard and hides a sentimental side.

Ice King

The lonely wizard with magical ice powers who just wants friends (and princesses).

The lovable little robot who is part video game console, part buddy, and full of life!
