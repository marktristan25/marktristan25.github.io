MacBook Pro 16
Unmatched Power, Unrivaled Creativity

Meet the MacBook Pro 16 – your ultimate creative powerhouse, designed for both the studio and on the go. Its Liquid Retina XDR display offers incredible brightness and color accuracy for stunning visuals, while a variety of Thunderbolt 4 ports provide seamless connectivity for all your devices. This MacBook is powered by the M2 Pro or M2 Max chip, delivering unparalleled performance with up to 12 CPU cores and 38 GPU cores, enabling you to tackle even the most demanding tasks. Additionally, it features up to 96GB of unified memory and up to 8TB of super-fast SSD storage, ensuring that you have all the space and speed you need for your creative projects. With exceptional battery life, a robust cooling system, and advanced features like ProMotion technology, the MacBook Pro 16 redefines what’s possible in a portable workstation.

KEY FEATURES

Processor: Apple M3 Pro/Max chip

The Apple M3 Pro and M3 Max chips are powerful processors designed for high performance and efficiency. They enable the MacBook Pro to handle demanding tasks like video editing and 3D rendering with ease.

Display: 16-inch Liquid Retina XDR display

A high-resolution screen that provides vibrant colors and sharp images, making everything look stunning, from movies to design work.

Battery Life: Up to 22 hours

The MacBook can last for an entire day on a single charge, allowing you to work or play without worrying about finding a power outlet.

Memory and Storage: Up to 128GB unified memory and up to 8TB SSD storage

This allows for exceptional performance and ample space to store your projects, ensuring you can handle even the most demanding applications without any slowdown.

Graphics: 16-core GPU, 19-core GPU, or 38-core GPU options

These options refer to the number of processing cores dedicated to graphics. More cores mean better performance in tasks like gaming, video editing, and graphic design.
